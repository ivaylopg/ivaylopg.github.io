Hello.

I'm **Ivaylo Getov**.

I'm an **Interactive Producer/Director** and Media Artist. I Specialize in **Technical Direction**, **Interaction Design**, and **Software Development** for public experiences that **use technology to bring people together** and have a good time.

In 2015 I co-founded [Luxloop](http://luxloop.com), a creative technology studio based in NY and LA where we brought our background as filmmakers to developing interactive stories with artists, museums, and brands.

Currently I'm Head of "Interactive" at [Dandelion + Burdock](https://dandelion-burdock.com/) and a wandering Technical Director for Fine Art projects

I was born in Sofia, Bulgaria and I have lived in Brooklyn, NY; Port Elizabeth, South Africa; and Havana, Cuba. I'm mostly based in Los Angeles. I am a US and EU Citizen, which comes in handy. I struggle with finding the best description or title for [what I do](whatido) because I don't think the perfect word exists yet. Some adjectives that have been used to describe me include: Director, Coder, Artist, Professor, Technical Director, Photographer, Designer, Programmer, Technologist, Producer, Game Master, Consultant, and Karaoke Superstar.
