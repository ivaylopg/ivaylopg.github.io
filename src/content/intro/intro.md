Hello.

I'm **Ivaylo Getov**.

I am an Interactive Producer and Technical Director leading teams and productions at the intersection of technology and creative vision. I have over a decade of experience building **public experiences that use technology to bring people together** in museums, galleries, and live events around the world. 

I take responsibility for complex, technically demanding workflows, and I feel as comfortable writing a server backend or prototyping a sensor system as I am working with stakeholders or translating a technical constraint into a creative opportunity.

From 2015 to 2019 I was **Cofounder and Technology Director** at [Luxloop](http://luxloop.com), a creative technology studio in NY and LA, where I led technical development on interactive storytelling projects.

From 2019 to 2024 I was **Head of Interactive** at [dandelion+burdock](https://dandelion-burdock.com/), a London-based company creating content and systems for LED, Projection, and Real-Time Environments.

Also from 2019 until right now, I have been a **Technical Director** and **Interactive Producer** at [Pixels Pixels](http://pixelspixels.com), producing and supporting interactive installations in the fine-art world for globally renowned artists and institutions.

I was born in Sofia, Bulgaria and I have lived in Brooklyn, NY; Port Elizabeth, South Africa; and Havana, Cuba. I'm mostly based in Los Angeles. I am a US and EU Citizen, which comes in handy. I struggle with finding the best description or title for [what I do](whatido) because I don't think the perfect word exists yet, but it has been a joy watching the world of creative technology evolve. Some adjectives that have been used to describe me include: Technical Director, Producer, Programmer, Artist, Professor, Technologist, Game Master, and Karaoke Enthusiast.
